About Me

Hi, I'm Enrique Gonzalez, a SoC Design Engineer at Intel, and I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Puerto Rico-Mayaguez. During my studies, I concentrated on both hardware and software systems

My work blends hardware and software expertise to create innovative solutions. I have experience across various domains, including RTL design, physical design, embedded systems, web applications, and AI research. This diverse background allows me to tackle complex challenges from multiple perspectives, ensuring seamless integration between hardware and software. Whether it's designing efficient digital circuits, developing embedded solutions, building intuitive web applications, or conducting AI research, I strive to deliver well-rounded, optimized results.

I also place a strong emphasis on project management skills and applying industrial psychology concepts to enhance team dynamics. I believe that achieving goals goes hand-in-hand with improving team confidence and ensuring everyone is empowered to perform at their best. My passion for effective teamwork and strong communication helps drive successful project outcomes.

Currently, I'm pursuing a master's degree in AI at Johns Hopkins University to further advance my knowledge and expertise in applying AI to digital design. I'm also learning more about advanced computer architectures, including spiking neural network architectures. My goal is to push the boundaries of what's possible in the semiconductor industry, using AI to streamline design processes and bring innovative products to life.
